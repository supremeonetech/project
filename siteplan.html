Campus Study Hub - Website Plan

Site Name

Campus Study Hub – A productivity and planning tool designed for UNIZIK students. The name reflects the core focus of helping students succeed in their academic environment.

Optional Domain: campusstudyhub.org

Site Purpose

The site is designed to help students identify good reading locations on campus, stay motivated with uplifting quotes, and manage their reading timetables. It will also serve as a hub for study group coordination and productivity tips.

Scenarios

  • Where are the best quiet places to study on campus?
  • How can I organize my reading timetable for the semester?

Color Schema

  • Primary: #004080 – Used for headers and navigation
  • Secondary: #f0f8ff – Used for background and content sections

Typography

  • Heading Font: Roboto, sans-serif – Used for all major headings
  • Body Font: Arial, sans-serif – Used for main content and paragraphs
